The Triad of Modern Defense

Traditional SOCs rely heavily on static rules which lead to "Alert Fatigue"—hundreds of false alarms that hide the real threats. NativeSOC reimagines this by layering three distinct types of intelligence to filter noise and focus on actionable threats.

Artificial Intelligence

The first line of defense. AI processes massive volumes of log data instantly to correlate events across your entire infrastructure.

  • 24/7 Automated Monitoring
  • Instant Data Correlation
  • Zero-Latency Decision Making

Machine Learning

The adaptive engine. ML establishes a "baseline" of normal behavior for your specific network and flags anomalies.

  • User Behavior Analytics (UEBA)
  • Adapts to New Threat Patterns
  • Reduces False Positives

Human Intelligence

The strategic mind. Expert analysts investigate the high-fidelity alerts that AI and ML have filtered and prioritized.

  • Complex Threat Hunting
  • Incident Response Strategy
  • Ethical Hacking Expertise
