How It Works

From ingestion to remediation, the NativeSOC lifecycle.

1
Ingest

Logs and events are collected from Endpoints, Cloud, Network, and Apps via secure agents.

2
Analyze

Data is normalized. The AI engine correlates events against 50,000+ detection rules.

3
Detect

4
Respond
